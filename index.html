HOW IT WORKS

Up and running
in minutes.

01

Download & Install

Click the download button, run the installer, and Wavecue sets itself up. A shortcut appears on your desktop automatically.

02

Upload Your Sounds

Click any empty pad to open your file picker, or just drag and drop audio files directly onto the pads. Supports MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, AAC, and more.

03

Assign Hotkeys

Click SHORTCUTS on any pad and press your desired key combo. Works globally — trigger sounds from any app or game.

04

Play & Have Fun

Click a pad or press your hotkey — the sound plays instantly. Customize colors, animations, and effects to make your board unique.

🎮

Make your friends hear it too.

With a free virtual audio cable, everyone in your Discord call or game lobby will hear your sounds loud and clear.

1
Download & install VB-Cable (free at vb-audio.com)
2
In Wavecue Settings → Secondary Output → CABLE Input
3
In Discord → Voice Settings → Input → CABLE Output
4
Turn off Echo Cancellation & Noise Suppression in Discord
5
Play a sound — everyone hears it! 🎉
